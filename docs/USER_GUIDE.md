# LeadOS Field Executive User Guide

Welcome to LeadOS! This guide will help you navigate the app and hit your daily targets efficiently.

## 1. Getting Started
LeadOS is designed to work completely **offline**. If you lose internet connection in the field, continue working as normal. LeadOS will save all your updates and automatically sync them to the server when your connection returns.

## 2. Daily Workflow
Your workflow revolves around the **Dashboard** and the **Priority Queue**.

### The Dashboard
When you open LeadOS, the dashboard tells you exactly what to do next:
- **Today's Mission:** A checklist of targets for the day (e.g., Collect Walk-ins, Complete Trainings).
- **Upcoming Follow-ups:** A quick summary of who needs to be contacted today.
- **Add Contact:** The large floating button at the bottom allows you to quickly capture new leads.

### The Priority Queue
Located in the Navigation Bar, the Queue intelligently orders your tasks. 
- It bubbles up **Urgent** tasks (like pending recharges or overdue follow-ups) and **High Potential** candidates.
- Tap a card in the queue to instantly call or WhatsApp them.

## 3. Managing Contacts
Every person you interact with is a **Contact**. 
- A Contact can be an Opportunity (Candidate), a Referral Partner, or an Influencer.
- To view a Contact's timeline, open their profile from the Network tab. You will see every call, WhatsApp, and status change logged chronologically.
- Use the sticky action bar at the bottom of a profile to instantly log a Call or schedule a Follow-up.

## 4. End of Day
At the end of your shift, go to the Dashboard and click the **End Day** button.
1. LeadOS will calculate your daily statistics (Walk-ins, Screenings, Incentives).
2. It will automatically shift any unfinished follow-ups to tomorrow morning so nothing is lost.
3. It will generate a summarized report that you can instantly share via WhatsApp with your team lead.

## Keyboard Shortcuts (Tablet/Desktop)
If you are using a Bluetooth keyboard or working from a laptop, use these shortcuts:
- `N` : Quick add new contact
- `F` : Open the Priority Queue
- `Cmd + K` : Global Search
- `Cmd + Enter` : Save a form
